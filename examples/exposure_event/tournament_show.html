Important- Must Read!

Respect the Game
1. No aggressive behavior or abusive language within the facility; failure to comply will result in your prompt removal...DO NOT COME, IF YOU ARE UNABLE TO CONTROL YOURSELF
2. No Fighting! Failure to comply will result in player removal and possible team removal. Adult fighting will result in Big Time Hoops seeking a lawful resolution. If, you are unable to control yourself ---- STAY HOME

Game Rules

· TEAMS MUST REMOVE ALL DEBRIS AFTER EACH GAME

· Clock Stops on Free-Throws, Fouls or Stoppage of play exceeding 5 seconds (Clock will stop on Whistle, the final minute of each half)

· 16 mins per half (Ages 15U – 18U / 9th – 12th Grade)

· 14 mins per half (Ages 13U – 14U / 7th & 8th Grade)

· 13 mins per half (Ages 9U – 12U / 3rd -6th Grade)

· 2 Min halftime

· One full and one 30 sec time out per half

· Overtime consist of 2 min, each team having one full timeout; sudden death in 3rd Overtime (first team to score)

· Free throws are "ON THE RELEASE”

· Each player has 5 fouls before disqualification

· DOUBLE BONUS on 10th foul (no one in one on 7th foul)

· Only 1 coach can stand during game

· *IF NEEDED* Home team will provide official score keeper with visiting team keeping clock. TEAM DEPICTED ON LEFT OR TOP OF SCHEDULE IS HOME.

· High School Federation Rules apply for all other scenarios

Slaughter Rule: Clock runs when opposing team is trailing by 20 points. Clock will stop on whistle, when deficit reaches 15 or below.

Tickets / Wristbands

Spectators must wear wristband on person; they are not a family bands but an individual band. Failure to have on person will result in repayment.

Coaches must have their wristband on person the entire weekend. Lead by example and keep your band on your wrist.

Team Check-In

Coaches/Teams must check in at the venue of their first game. No coaching band will be provided if you go to the wrong location before your first game.

Team Rosters

Those that failed to submit a team roster at the HS level will only hurt their players. We have college coaches in attendance and watching online, get the roster in by 8pm tomorrow. An email has been sent to those teams out of compliance.

General Information

Big Time Hoops is proud to host the Circuit of Champions basketball tournament in Southern Chicago. This premier basketball tournament is open to all boys or girls basketball organizations from ages 9U - 18U. This is a 4 game guarantee event with pool play on Saturday and bracket play on Sunday.

Qualifier for the Big Time Hoops National Championships

For 2022, the National Championships will be held on Jul 1st – 3rd in Philadelphia, PA. Host location will be the Greater Philadelphia Expo Center!

$250 per Team (Automatic Bid) – **placed 1st or 2nd Place in Gold Bracket or 1st in Silver of previous Big Time Hoops multi day event (Sept 1, 2021 – Jun 26, 2022)

$450 per Team – Limited to the first 50 Teams! Register Early!

4 Game Guarantee with Pool to Bracket Play. All games under one roof from Friday through Sunday. Compete against the top programs from across the nation.

BALLERTV will cover all the action!

Big Time Hoops relies on each team's assessment to properly place teams in the appropriate pools. This will allow for more competitive games and a high energy atmosphere for players, coaches and spectators.

Skill Level Breakdown:

  • "A" level teams - National Travel that has played in multiple championship games.
  • "B" level teams - Team that has won bracket/playoff games at Regional level tournaments.
