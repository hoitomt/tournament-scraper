6/3/2022 8:52 AM
HH Player Check-In Due

Hello Hardwood Hustle Participants,

We are excited to see you compete this weekend and continue to develop on this great journey through sports performance!

We have some great features this year, including more sweet awards for the 1st and 2nd place winners of each Division, Special Apparel, and Special Bag-Tags for every player.

APPAREL
You can pre-order apparel through our local vendor Fine Designs partnered with Blaze. All apparel can be pre-ordered or ordered at the event this weekend!

PLAYER CHECK-IN
This is a Stay-to-Play event and the Fox Valley Visitor's Bureau as well as our Travel Partner, Site Search, welcomes you! Since this is a stay-to-play event, ALL participants coming in from outside the Fox Valley Area (outside of Green Bay to Oshkosh) are required to fill out a Player Check-In Form. These player check-in forms must be completed before your Club Coach checks in. Having all players checked in will make his/her check-in process run much more smoothly. Coaches check-in table will be at the court level of the Champion Center and they will at that time receive bag-tags for each player.

The Player Check-In Form will ask for your information about one of the following:

1) Your Site-Search Hotel
2) Your NON Site-Search Hotel
3) Airbnb/VRBO Residence
4) Commuter Details

This is a short survey that will only take a couple minutes. Please have your details ready such as name of lodging, address, and confirmation #.

If you have any questions regarding lodging or still need a place to reside this weekend, please reach out to Site Search at admin@sitesearchllc.com

SCHEDULE
Please keep track of your schedule using the Blaze Events app up to game time. We still are finalizing this evening, the impact of a boys' and a girls' 16u team backing out this week.

MEDIA, COACHES, and PLAYERS
We will be using our Blaze Sports and Big Bark Marketing staff to manage all Media this weekend. We will be posting special POWER PLAYERannouncements during the event and ALL STARS after the event. Please make sure that all scorekeepers obtain All-Star votes after each game from both team coaches. You will find this form on the back of each scoresheet.

All social media announcements can be found on our Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter Hardwood Hustle pages. Please tag each page for added impact and use the hashtag #HHPowerPlayer as well!

LIVE STREAMING
All games will be Live Streamed through BallerTV!

CODE OF CONDUCT
This year we are keeping it simple:
No Negativity Allowed!
Positive Parents Only

We ask all participants including spectators, coaches, and players act, compete, and represent their programs and families with respect and positivity.

We believe that we will have so many Positive Parents in the building and look forward to reveling and sharing them and the Power Players they have raised!

WELCOME EMAIL
You can find the complete Welcome Email using this LINK!

We look forward to seeing you compete well this weekend!

Respectfully!
Lisa and Blaze Staff

6/13/2022 6:32 PM
Hardwood Hustle Thank You

Dear Directors, Coaches, and Families!

We are sending a HUGE THANK YOU! for participating in our Blaze Wisconsin Hardwood Hustle tournament this past weekend.

Thank you to adjusting to the last minute schedule changes. We apologize for any inconvenience. Those unforseen last minute drop-outs make it tough. We had about 270 games this weekend and things went pretty smoothly in light of that!

We heard some great compliments and that people are definitely coming back next year. Thank you for sharing your positive feedback.

Thank you for filling out the "Player Check-In" form. It was new for us and helpful. We noticed we needed to tweak the form a little bit so for the first entries, we apologize if you answered more questions than necessary!

We have posted all our in tourney "Power Player" graphics on social media. By early next week we will have the All-Star results posted as well. You can find all these great updates on Instagram, Facebook, and Twitter on our Hardwood Hustle pages.

If you would like to purchase any more apparel from Fine Designs, you can find their online store at FINE DESIGNS

We will send one more email when the All-Stars are posted.

If you haven't yet registered your teams for Recruit Wisconsin, make sure to do so before registration closes. We have extended registration to Monday, June 13th! This will be another great event with college coaches in the house!

Thank you and we look forward to seeing you soon!

Lisa and our Awesome Blaze Staff!
